# What WALTER Can Do

## Voice & Telegram Control
Listens via Telegram (text or voice message). Whisper transcribes speech to text.

## System Control
Volume, mute, screenshot, lock screen, minimize/maximize/close windows, media play/pause/next/prev. Works in English and Turkish.

## App & Window Management
Open any installed app, switch focus between running windows, close specific apps by name.

## Keyboard & Mouse
Any hotkey combo, individual keys, scroll, click, double-click, right-click, type text into any window.

## Agentic Planning
For anything complex, an LLM builds a multi-step execution plan and runs it — searching the web, extracting values, chaining results between steps.

## Web & YouTube
- Search DuckDuckGo and extract specific facts
- Find and play any YouTube video directly (no results page)
- Open any URL in Firefox
- Search any site (YouTube, Reddit, Google, etc.) and show results

## File Operations
Find and open files by partial name across Desktop, Documents, Downloads.

## AI Queries
Ask Claude or Gemini a question, get the answer back in Telegram. Generate images via Gemini.

## Clipboard
Read from or write to clipboard mid-plan.

## Auto Feedback
After every action, takes a screenshot of the screen and sends it back. On error, same.
